Value of Linkberry Token

The value of Linkberry tokens increases as the number of Linkberry app users and viewers of the Yabak Studio YouTube channel grows. This is because a portion of the ad revenue generated from both the app and the YouTube channel will be used to buy back tokens once a month.

Transparent Donation

Is Linkberry Legitimately Bridging The Financial Gap?



Self-Sustaining Economic Model

Instead of donating money directly, donors can purchase Linkberry tokens, which increases the value of the tokens, benefiting both donors and those in need. The poor receive tokens that they can either trade on exchanges or use for other purposes, making the process mutually beneficial and potentially more sustainable.

Incentivizing Value Growth

Donors’ purchases of Linkberry tokens help drive the value of the tokens up. This creates an incentive for donors to contribute because the rising value of the tokens also benefits those in need. It becomes a self-reinforcing cycle where both sides have a vested interest in the success of the platform.

Transparency

If the distribution of tokens and how they benefit the poor can be clearly tracked, it builds trust among donors. The growth of Linkberry and the increasing token value would directly correlate with the benefits received by the impoverished, encouraging more people to participate.

ISPO

A fair, safe and revolutionary fundraising mechanism only on Cardano Blockchain

What is ISPO?

An ISPO is a revolutionary new fundraising mechanism that is more fair, inclusive, secure. It is a unique financing tool that allows the community to support their favorite project without investing their own assets, but instead leveraging Cardano’s native reward incentive structure.A portion of the ADA rewards obtained through ISPO will be used for donations to an orphanage in Nigeria and for a program to support content creators in Kenya. Additionally, it will be used for the buyback of LINKE tokens listed on CEX exchanges.

How to participate in ISPO?

- Connect with your selected Wallet (Ethernl, etc)
- Confirm you are delegating to $LIBE or $Yabak
- Sign the delegation transaction
- Enjoy Staking with Linkberry!

Stake in $LIBE

Anyone who stakes a minimum of 1,000 ADA in the LIBE pool will receive 0.5 LINKE tokens per ADA as a reward for each epoch. Tokens will be distributed to all $LIBE pool stakers every three months.

Stake in $Yabak

Anyone who stakes a minimum of 1,000 ADA in the YABAK pool will receive 0.05 LINKE tokens per ADA as a reward for each epoch. The Stakers can claim LINKE tokens in Vending Machine.

History

Empowering lives through digital value.

  • 2023

    Ideation Linkberry Project

    With the advisory support of South Korean fintech company T, we designed the Linkberry project to implement international charitable initiatives on the Cardano blockchain! Conducting patent and trademark registration activities in South Korea.

  • The first half of 2024

    Design of Linkberry

    The Linkberry app is designed to provide financial assistance to people in need, particularly in developing countries. By leveraging Cardano blockchain technology, it enables transparent and efficient distribution of funds while allowing users to earn rewards through various activities. Ultimately, the app aims to empower individuals by helping them generate income and become influencers within the ecosystem.

  • The second half of 2024

    ISPO and Linkberry App Launching

    The Linkberry ISPO (Initial Stake Pool Offering) is a community-driven fundraising mechanism that allows participants to support the Linkberry project by staking their ADA in designated Cardano stake pools. Instead of traditional fundraising methods, ISPO enables users to delegate their ADA without losing ownership while receiving Linkberry tokens (LINKE) as rewards.

  • 2025

    The official launch of project activities

    Starting in 2025, Linkberry will focus on expanding its ecosystem, increasing user adoption, and enhancing financial aid distribution through Cardano blockchain technology.
